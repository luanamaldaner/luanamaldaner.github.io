Overview

As a Digitization Intern at the Florida Museum of Natural History, I work with the herbarium's extensive collection of plant specimens. The position involves careful handling, documentation, and digitization of botanical specimens to make them accessible to researchers worldwide.

What I Do

  • Specimen Curation — Handling and organizing plant specimens according to scientific standards
  • Digital Photography — Capturing high-quality images for digital archives
  • Database Management — Entering specimen information into research databases
  • Mounting & Filing — Properly mounting specimens on herbarium sheets

Skills Developed

Scientific Photography Data Management Specimen Handling Digital Archiving Research Methods

Connection to BME

The systematic data management, attention to detail, and documentation skills from this position directly apply to biomedical engineering. Experience with digital imaging and databases provides a foundation for medical imaging technologies and health informatics.
