Mosquito Tracker

App Improvement Track Winner

An app to track and report mosquito populations, helping communities prevent the spread of mosquito-borne diseases.

Winner

The Problem

Mosquito-borne diseases like dengue, Zika, and malaria affect millions of people worldwide. Early detection and tracking of mosquito populations can help communities take preventive action before outbreaks occur.

The Solution

Mosquito Tracker is a mobile app that allows users to report mosquito sightings and breeding grounds in their area. The data is aggregated to create heat maps showing high-risk areas, helping local health departments and communities take targeted action.

Features

  • Report Sightings - Quick and easy way to log mosquito activity
  • Heat Maps - Visual representation of mosquito density by area
  • Alerts - Notifications for high-risk areas nearby
  • Prevention Tips - Educational content on reducing breeding grounds

Technologies

Mobile Development Geolocation Data Visualization API Integration
